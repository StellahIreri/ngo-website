Welcome to African Diaspora Youth

Our Mission

To empower African diaspora youth in America by fostering resilience, promoting mental wellness, and equipping them with the tools and support needed to navigate the challenges of cultural identity, family dynamics, educational aspirations, and healthy living.

Our Vision

Our nonprofit exists to empower African diaspora youth in America to overcome challenges that stem from cultural heritage, peer pressure, and social influences, including the risks of crime and substance misuse. We strive to build confidence and resilience, equipping youth to stand strong and pursue their dreams, even when faced with adversity.
